I am Tanya Gandhi, dedicated UX designer and researcher whose journey has been shaped by the unexpected twists of life and a passion for merging neuroscience insights with user-centric design.

A Twist of Fate: I started my academic journey in biomedical sciences, eventually diving into the field of neuroscience for my Ph.D., exploring the mysteries of the brain. However, my journey took an unforeseen turnâ€”a moment etched in time when a finger nerve accident while performing lab experiment reshaped the narrative of my life. This unexpected incident was more than a detour; it became the compass guiding me towards a new professional chapter.

Rethinking Priorities: The incident compelled me to confront the fragility of life and prompted a deep reflection on my professional journey. As I navigated the challenges of recovery, I realized the profound impact that design, empathy, and user-centric thinking could have on improving everyday experiences. It's important to note that I am fully recovered and capable of any job, yet I chose UX design as my path to channel my resilience and newfound insights into creating meaningful and impactful designs.

Bridging the Gap: My transition from neuroscience to UX design is fueled by a desire to bridge the gap between scientific research and human-centric design. The skills honed in the laboratory, such as precision, attention to detail, and analytical thinking, seamlessly translate into my approach to crafting intuitive and accessible user experiences.

UX Design: A Natural Evolution: Choosing UX design was not a departure from neuroscience; rather, it was an evolution, where I found a fulfilling way to channel my scientific curiosity into designing solutions that positively impact people's lives. I realized the potential to leverage my deep understanding of cognitive processes to craft interfaces that not only function seamlessly but also enhance the overall user experience.

Human-Centered Passion: I am driven by a passion for creating technology that not only meets functional requirements but also enhances the overall well-being and satisfaction of users. My experiences have instilled in me a deep empathy for the end-users, and I am dedicated to championing their needs through thoughtful and inclusive design.

Embracing Change: My story is a testament to the transformative power of change and the unexpected paths that lead to fulfilling careers. Let's connect and explore how my diverse background and resilient spirit can bring a fresh perspective to your design projects.
